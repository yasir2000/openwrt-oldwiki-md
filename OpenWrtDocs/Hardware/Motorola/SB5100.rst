== Serial pinout ==

There is an unpopulated U12 connector, here is the pinout :

{{{

  ---  --- VCC
  ---  ---
  ---  --- GND
  ---  ---
  ---  ---
  ---  --- TX
  ---  ---
RX---  ---
}}}

== JTAG pinout ==

The 10-pin JTAG pinout is as follow :

{{{
Ethernet connector

TCK  TMS  TDO  TDI  TRST
GND  GND  GND  GND  GND

USB connector
}}}

= Serial port =
The FVX538 has a serial port with its DB9 connector. Levels are 0-5V (null modem cable required).

Serial parameters:
||Speed || 115200 bauds ||
||Data bits || 8 ||
||Stop bits || 1 ||
||Parity || none ||
||Flow control || none ||

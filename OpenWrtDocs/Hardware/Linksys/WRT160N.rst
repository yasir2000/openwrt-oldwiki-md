= Linksys WRT160N =

The Linksys WRT160N is the Ultra RangePlus Wireless-N Broadband Router.

== Hardware ==

=== Chipset ===

 * BCM4703
 * BCM4321L
 * BCM5325

=== Flashchip ===

 * EN29LV320AB

=== Wireless Chip ===

 * BCM2055 (under the shield)
